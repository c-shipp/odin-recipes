Baked Ziti Recipe

Ahhhhh pasta! It's Italian night and there's no better dish to bake than this Baked Ziti!

In my home nothing makes me happier than the aroma of a rich spaghetti sauce stewing on the stove. The sizzle of ground beef on the pan. The tears from chopping onions! Okay, maybe not the tears.. but for this dish it's worth it! Nothing says italian night quite like Baked Ziti.

Whether you're feeding yourself or you've decided to cook for your family and friends, Ziti should be a go to dish. The ingredients are inexpensive and will go a long ways to feeding those you care about. Now let's jump right in so you can get that first crisp al dente bite.

    Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
