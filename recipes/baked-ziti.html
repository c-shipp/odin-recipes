Baked Ziti Recipe

Ahhhhh pasta! It's Italian night and there's no better dish to bake than this Baked Ziti!

In my home nothing makes me happier than the aroma of a rich spaghetti sauce stewing on the stove. The sizzle of ground beef on the pan. The tears from chopping onions! Okay, maybe not the tears.. but for this dish it's worth it! Nothing says italian night quite like Baked Ziti.

Whether you're feeding yourself or you've decided to cook for your family and friends, Ziti should be a go to dish. The ingredients are inexpensive and will go a long ways to feeding those you care about. Now let's jump right in so you can get that first crisp al dente bite.

    Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

    Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Brings a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    3. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spagetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degreees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Spread ½ of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish
  5. Top with Provolone cheese
  6. Top with Sour Cream
  7. Add ½ of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and then remaining meat sauce. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until heated through and cheeses have melted, about 30 minutes.
