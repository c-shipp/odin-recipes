Stop buying those $0.99 Swanson Chicken Pot Pies and upgrade your life with this terrific from-scratch Pot Pie dish

This pot pie dish is moist, rich, and ultra-flavorful with bread-crumbs that subsitute for pie crust Pot pie is your quintessential southern comfort food and we're ready to show you how to elevate this dish.
